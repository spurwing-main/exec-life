The challenge

Aethos had outgrown a fragmented WordPress setup, with separate sites for each hotel, ageing localisation, inconsistent integrations and too much reliance on developers for everyday change. The new website needed to bring the brand website, The Club and seven destinations into one unified Webflow build, without losing the character, content depth and operational needs of each location.

Our approach

Working as Grotesk’s Webflow development partner, we translated their creative direction into a flexible, component-led Webflow system. The build brought Aethos’ brand, destination and club content into a more manageable structure, supported by reusable CMS collections, responsive components, considered animation and the integrations needed for experiences, enquiries and booking journeys.
